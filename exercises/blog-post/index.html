F1 Bahrain Testing Day 2 Results

by Arron Jones•Feb 12,2026

The F1 Bahrain Testing Day 2 action continued on Thursday morning, with Ferraris Charles Leclerc setting the pace during the second day of the first official pre-season test of the 2026 Formula 1 season.

Under clear skies at the Bahrain International Circuit, teams continued to gather vital data under the all-new 2026 regulations. But it was Leclerc who emerged quickest in the morning running, laying down an early marker for Ferrari.

Leclerc clocked a fastest time of 1:34.273, finishing comfortably clear at the top of the timesheets. McLarens Lando Norris was second, 0.511 seconds adrift, after completing the highest lap tally of the session with 64 tours — two more than both Leclerc and Williams Alex Albon.
